Darwin

The card art below was half of the card art for a project in Game Design 1 where we had to create a party game. Our game was called "Darwin" and was based on evolving these fish and fighting enemies and bosses, the numbers on the cards being their strength. The lines on the cards would be cut, so there would 5 strips of each fish, and they would be "Frankensteined" together.
